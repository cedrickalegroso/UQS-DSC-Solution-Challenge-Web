How it works?

Register your service.

By registering and setting up your service out of the box you will have a queue system already. If you already have an existing queue system and still want to use UQS dont worry we got you covered! you can use our API . Read more about this in our documentation .

Start using UQS.

Start your UQS service it will handle the rest for you. you can now go to specific cashier in your service then click start accepting queue.

Tada! Hassless Queue!

Customers will see your Queue status online using the UQS mobile app and they can create a ticket for them selves. dont worry just focus on your thing UQS will handle the rest.
